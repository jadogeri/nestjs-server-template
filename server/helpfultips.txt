Below is a comprehensive list of real-world examples categorized by their purpose:
1. Data Maintenance & Cleanup
Session/Token Cleanup: Deleting expired JWT tokens or session records to keep the database small and fast.
Soft-Delete Purging: Permanently removing records that were "soft-deleted" more than 30 days ago to comply with GDPR or data retention policies.
Log Rotation: Archiving or deleting application logs older than a specific timeframe to save disk space.
Temporary File Removal: Deleting old PDF reports or uploaded images from a tmp/ folder after they have been processed or emailed.

Subscription Reminders: Checking for users whose subscriptions expire in 3 days and sending a "Renewal Soon" email.
